About Us

ID Sahil Motwani

Founder & Principal Designer

Sahil Motwani is the Founder and Principal Designer of RBS Design Studio, where he leads a diverse range of residential and commercial projects. His design philosophy centers on the belief that "spaces tell stories and design shapes experiences," a mantra that drives the studio to blend creativity
With extensive experience across Chhattisgarh and Mumbai, Sahil’s portfolio includes high-profile hospitality work, such as the design of the banquet, landscape, and swimming pool for Hotel Kings Mahal in Bemetara. His expertise extends to specialized commercial environments, including jewelry showrooms and CA offices, as well as high-profile architectural planning and project support for developments associated with the Ambani group and NTPC-related work.

Crafting Spaces, Transforming Lives

At RBS Design Studio, we believe great design is not just about creating beautiful spaces — it is about crafting experiences that reflect personality, purpose, and lifestyle. With a passion for luxury interiors and timeless aesthetics, we specialize in designing sophisticated residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces that blend elegance with functionality.

Our approach is driven by creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence. Every project is thoughtfully curated to achieve a seamless balance of modern design, comfort, and practicality. From concept development to final execution, we focus on delivering spaces that feel refined, distinctive, and truly personalized.
